The generated project must be a runnable bubbletea v2 hello-world.

Purpose: Prove the full scaffolder pipeline (CLI parse → embed → render → emit → verify) works against real charmbracelet v2 packages. Every later plan builds on this skeleton without changing its architectural decisions (see `01-SKELETON.md`).
Output: A working `spin` binary plus a `01-SKELETON.md` recording architectural decisions for subsequent phases.
</objective>
